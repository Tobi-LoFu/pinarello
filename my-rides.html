MY RIDES

Hey guys, most of the time I'm recording my rides using a GPS bike computer and my mobile phone. Then after the ride I'm gonna import the GPS track file to komoot and can easyly check the duration, the length, average speed and some other data like the elevation and speed profile of the track. Together with other Apps on my mobile phone like Strava or Training Peaks I can evaluate my workout. The more data the Apps will get e.g. by connecting a heart rate sensor or/and cadence sensor to the bike computer the more specific the evaluation. Those Apps can analyse the efficiency of your workout and give advices how to improve your training rides.
